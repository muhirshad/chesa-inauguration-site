About CHESA

CHESA (Computer Hardware Engineering Association) is the student community of the Computer Hardware Engineering Department at Government Polytechnic College, Palakkad. The association hosts talks, workshops, hackathons, and community projects that bridge hardware and software for real‑world impact.

  • Time: 8:00 AM (IST)
  • Venue: Main Auditorium, Government Polytechnic College, Palakkad

Why Attend?
